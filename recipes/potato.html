Fried Potato


Description

This recipe will show you how easy cook delicious fried potato.
All what ypu need some ingredients, like potato, oil and spices.

Full cooking takes 35 mins. It will be enough for 4 people.

Ingredients

  • 2 medium russet potatoes
  • 1 ½ tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon ground paprika
  • ½ teaspoon parsley flakes
  • ½ teaspoon chili powder
  • ½ teaspoon sea salt
  • ⅛ teaspoon ground black pepper

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients. Preheat an air fryer to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Cut each potato in half lengthwise. Cut each half in half lengthwise, and then cut each quarter in half lengthwise.
    You will have 16 wedges. Gather the seasonings.
  3. Place potato wedges in a large bowl. Add olive oil, paprika, parsley, chili, salt,
    and pepper; mix until well combined.
  4. Place 1/2 of the potato wedges in the basket of the air fryer and cook for 10 minutes.
  5. Flip wedges with tongs and cook for an additional 5 minutes. Remove to a plate. Repeat to cook the remaining wedges.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy!
